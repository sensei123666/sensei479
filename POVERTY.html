Poverty

What is poverty?

Poverty is the state of not having enough material possessions or income for a person's basic needs. Poverty may include social, economic, and political elements. Absolute poverty is the complete lack of the means necessary to meet basic personal needs, such as food, clothing, and shelter. The solutions are supporting pay equity. creating jobs and establishing work schedules that are convienient.

How can we reduce poverty?

We can reduce poverty by the following

  • We can have more schools made for increased education
  • We can Increase employment.
  • We can Accelerate Economic Growth
  • We can Speed developement of infastructure
